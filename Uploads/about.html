About MindSpace

A student-built platform dedicated to making mental health support accessible to every university student.

Our Mission

Making student wellbeing a priority

At Mbarara University of Science and Technology, we saw fellow students struggling silently — with exam pressure, loneliness, financial stress, and academic expectations. MindSpace was born from that observation.

Our goal is to build a safe, accessible digital space where any student can check in with their emotions, find support, and know they are not alone.

How It Works

Getting started is simple

  1. 1

    Visit the Platform

    Open MindSpace on any device — phone, tablet, or computer. No installation needed.

  2. 2

    Check In With Your Mood

    Log how you're feeling using the Mood Tracker. Takes under a minute.

  3. 3

    Write in Your Journal

    Open your private journal and write freely. Guided prompts available if you need a start.

  4. 4

    Reach Out When Needed

    Connect with a counselor or access emergency contacts through the Contact page.

Key Facts

What every student should know

  • Mental health challenges are more common than academic failure — yet far less discussed.
  • Stigma remains the biggest barrier to students seeking support at universities in Uganda.
  • Regular journaling and mood tracking have been proven to reduce anxiety levels.
  • Community and peer connection significantly improve mental wellness outcomes.
  • Early intervention — even digital self-care tools — can prevent serious mental health crises.
